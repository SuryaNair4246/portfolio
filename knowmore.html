ABOUT ME

I am a skilled and creative Conversation Designer with 6+ years of experience designing intelligent IVR (Interactive Voice Response) systems and AI-powered chatbots. My passion lies in shaping engaging, user-centric conversational interfaces that bridge the gap between technology and human interaction. By focusing on the customer experience and leveraging data-driven insights, I create systems that improve efficiency, drive user satisfaction, and elevate business outcomes.

  • IVR Systems: Designing and optimizing user flows for phone-based customer service systems.

  • Voice User Interface (VUI): Designing conversational interfaces that feel natural and human on voice platforms.
