IVR Design and Voice flow for CapitalOne credit card applications

Client Overview

Capital One is the second-largest issuer in the country with 126.5 million credit cards. With close to 900,000 incoming calls each day, Capital one needed a world class IVR to handle such enormous call volume and provide customers with a seamless, efficient way to manage their accounts through automated voice prompts.

Challenges

Capital One's traditional IVR system for credit card applications was outdated and relied heavily on menu-driven navigation. Customers experienced frustration due to lengthy, complex menus, slow response times, and challenges in reaching the appropriate department or representative. To address these issues, the company needed to redesign the IVR flow to: -

  • Reduce customer frustration 
  • Offer quicker, more intuitive access to services 
  • Ensure accessibility for all users
  • Ensure accessibility for all users,including those with disabilities
  • Integrate advanced self-service features, such as Natural Language Processing (NLP) capabilities.

Solution

  • Research & Discovery – Conducting frequent analysis of call logs to identify common reasons for customer calls, frequently asked questions, and points where customers abandoned or dropped off in the IVR system.
  • AI Integration with NLP – Incorporated AI-driven Natural Language Processing (NLP) into the IVR design to facilitate more natural, efficient, and user-friendly interactions. With NLP, the system could understand the intent behind a customer’s query, rather than merely transcribing their words.
  • By integrating cross-platform and omnichannel capabilities, along with analyzing a caller's previous interactions, we were able to predict the caller's intent, significantly enhancing the overall experience.
  • Enhanced Error Recovery – Improved the system’s ability to handle ambiguous or unclear inputs, ensuring better recovery from errors.
  • Personalized Experience – Streamlined the process by skipping irrelevant steps, providing a more tailored and efficient experience for each user.

Results

  • Enhanced Customer Satisfaction
  • Better Accessibility & Inclusivity
  • By integrating cross-platform and omnichannel capabilities, along with analyzing a caller's previous interactions, we were able to predict the caller's intent, significantly enhancing the overall experience.
  • Higher Customer Retention
  • Cost Savings by the reduction in live agent calls
