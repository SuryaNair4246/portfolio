GENI - LLM Chatbot creation using Azure GPT Models

Company Overview

[24]7.ai is a leading customer engagement platform that leverages AI-driven solutions to enhance customer service experiences across various industries. The goal was to create an intelligent, user-friendly chatbot leveraging Large Language Models (LLMs) that would enhance the customer experience and demonstrate [24]7.ai’s expertise in AI-powered solution.

Challenges

The challenge was to design and develop a chatbot that was both functional and engaging, leveraging cutting-edge AI technology. The chatbot needed to meet several specific goals:

  • Provide seamless, intelligent assistance to website visitors. 
  • Effectively showcase [24]7.ai's product offerings and capabilities. 
  • Support a range of interactions, from FAQs to more complex inquiries about the company’s solutions.
  • Create a conversational experience that felt natural, human-like, and brand-appropriate.

Solution

  • To start the design process, extensive user research was conducted. The team identified key personas such as: prospective clients,existing clients,general visitors etc.
  • Training the LLM: The chatbot was fine-tuned with domain-specific knowledge about 24/7.ai’s products, services, and customer support processes using RAG sources.
  • Prompt engineering was introduced as a key strategy in refining how the chatbot interacted with users.
  • Fine-tuning the LLM on domain-specific data for providing accurate and relevant responses, and continuous monitoring of performance to maintain a high standard of service.

Results

  • Increased Customer Satisfaction
  • Reduced Load on Human Agents
  • Cost Efficiency
  • Improved Customer Experience
